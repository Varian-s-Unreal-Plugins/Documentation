# Cog
`InputNexus` provides a `Cog` debugger out of the box.

![[Pasted image 20260331195959.png]]

This debugger allows you to make certain modifications to all registered `InputNexus` components and their `InputLinks` in the level.
Adding it to your window manager is very simple. I prefer to put the window into the `Input` category.

![[Pasted image 20260331200009.png]]
